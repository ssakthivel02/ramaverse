Sources

Last updated: July 2026

RamaVerse is designed around the Valmiki Ramayana structure and public-domain source principles.

Source framework

  • Primary tradition: Valmiki Ramayana.
  • English public-domain reference: Ralph T. H. Griffith where legally safe.
  • Tamil summaries and explanations: original RamaVerse content requiring human Tamil review.
  • Children’s content and life guidance: original educational adaptations.

Copyright position

No copyrighted modern translations, PDFs, commercial books, or website commentaries should be copied into the app without explicit licence permission.

Corrections

If you find an error in source attribution, translation, Tamil wording, or cultural explanation, contact support.
