Delete Account

Last updated: July 2026

You can request deletion of your RamaVerse account and associated app data.

Steps

  1. Open the RamaVerse app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the request.
  4. If app access is unavailable, send a request through the Contact page using your registered email.

What is deleted

Account profile, bookmarks, notes, preferences, and synced learning data linked to your account will be deleted where technically possible.

Retention

Some security, fraud-prevention, or legal logs may be retained for a limited period if required.
