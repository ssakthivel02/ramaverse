Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RamaVerse provides Ramayana-based cultural, devotional and educational learning. This policy explains what data may be collected by the app and website.

Data we may collect

  • Account details if sign-in is enabled, such as name, email and profile identifier.
  • App preferences such as language, bookmarks, notes, streak and saved lessons.
  • Device and diagnostic data required to improve stability.
  • AI search queries if the user chooses to use AI Guide.

How data is used

Data is used to provide app features, sync user preferences, improve learning experience, support account deletion, and maintain safety.

Children

Kids Mode is designed as family-friendly learning content. No ads are planned in the MVP. Parents should supervise children using online features.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the RamaVerse support email shown on the Contact page.
