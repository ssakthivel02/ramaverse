FAQ

Last updated: July 2026

Is RamaVerse a remedy app?

No. It is a Ramayana-based cultural, devotional and educational learning app.

Is Tamil the primary language?

Yes. Tamil is the primary language, with English and other languages planned.

Does AI give guaranteed answers?

No. AI provides explanations and reflections. It must not invent verses or guarantee outcomes.

Can children use RamaVerse?

Yes, Kids Mode is designed to be family-friendly, but parents should supervise online features.

Will the full Ramayana be available?

The app starts with curated MVP content and expands toward a fuller verse-by-verse database over time.
