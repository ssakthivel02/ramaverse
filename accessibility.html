Accessibility

Last updated: July 2026

RamaVerse aims to be accessible to users across devices, ages and reading abilities.

Accessibility goals

  • Readable contrast and scalable text
  • Keyboard-friendly web navigation
  • Alt text for meaningful images
  • Clear Tamil and English wording
  • Audio learning support
  • Mobile responsive layout

Feedback

If any feature is difficult to access, contact support with your device and browser details.
